Theo Epstein

The Drought Buster

Notable Accomplishments

  • Graduate of Yale University

  • Law Degree from the University of San Diego Law School

  • Became the youngest GM in the history of baseball in 2002 when the Boston Red Sox hired him at age 28

  • Helped the Red Sox break the "Curse of the Bambino" in 2004, when they won the World Series for the first time since 1918

  • Won a second World Series with the Red Sox in 2007

  • Became the President of Baseball Operations for the Chicago Cubs in 2011

  • Helped the Cubs break the "Curse of the Billy Goat" in 2016, when the team appeared in their first World Series since 1945 and won their first World Series since 1908

  • Has won multiple awards for his excellence as an MLB Executive
